What's on in Van Nuys

 

Nestled in California's San Fernando Valley, Van Nuys is a youthful, vibrant Los Angeles enclave that exemplifies the sun-soaked culture of Southern California. Hollywood luminaries such as Robert Redford and Marilyn Monroe once resided in Van Nuys, which is also home to an expanded presence for Signature Flight Support, following the recent acquisition of the assets of Maguire Aviation Group at Van Nuys Airport. With the sun shining almost everyday and the scenic California coastline nearby, we hope you have the chance to visit.

 
 
 
 

Top Attractions Near Van Nuys:

  1. Venice Beach: Where SoCal gets its cool. Bohemian Venice Beach marches to the beat of its own drum, attracting skateboarders, bodybuilders and beach bums alike. Eat like a local at the popular Figtree Café or drop by Small World Books for a quick read.

  2. Rodeo Drive: Take a walk along this pricey thoroughfare and feel like a Hollywood star, showcasing some of the most exclusive stores in LA.

  3. Runyon Canyon Park: Just a 20-minute drive south of Van Nuys is Runyon Canyon Park, one of LA's top nature retreats. Great for a hike or run with rewarding views of the Hollywood Hills.

  4. The Getty Center: Is the Getty Center a museum? How about an architectural wonder? Or maybe it's a world-class garden? Actually it's all three, plus more!

  5. Hollywood Walk of Fame: Give in to your guilty pleasure and visit one of the world's most famous sidewalks. See if you can spot the stamp of your favorite Hollywood celebrity.
 
 

VNY: Van Nuys Airport


Signature Flight Support is proud to announce our expansion at Van Nuys Airport. Catering to private, chartered and small commercial aircraft, this northern Los Angeles airstrip is one of the world's busiest general aviation airports. Offering less hassle than nearby airports like LAX and Ontario, celebrities, politicians and business executives frequent VNY because it offers unparalleled convenience and anonymity.

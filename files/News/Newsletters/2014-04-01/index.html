What's on in Kissimmee

 

Kissimmee is right in the middle of all the activities and attractions that Central Florida has to offer, just 20 minutes away from Disney World and about 30 minutes from downtown Orlando. A family-oriented destination known for its themed dinner theaters, swamp tours and outdoor activities, Kissimmee offers everything from an exciting evening in Old Town to a tranquil day at Lake Tohopekaliga, which the locals just call Toho.

 
 
 
 

Top Attractions in Kissimmee:

  1. Disney World: How close is Kissimmee to Walt Disney World? So close you'll have to dodge the crashing waves of Splash Mountain! From Kissimmee Gateway Airport you are about 15 minutes from the front gate of Disney World, Epcot and Hollywood Studios. It's a small world after all.

  2. Old Town: A shopper's paradise, Old Town in Kissimmee offers over 75 specialty shops, inviting restaurants and plenty of entertainment. Every weekend the cars are the stars in Old Town, where a classic car show features some of the smoothest rides around.

  3. Warbird Adventures & The Kissimmee Air Museum: Nostalgic for the aviation classics? Stop by the Kissimmee Air Museum and take a step back in time, viewing vintage aircraft on display. What's better than that? How about taking one out for a spin at Warbird Adventures, where you can fly like the aces of yesteryear.

  4. Florida EcoSafaris at Florida Forever: Journey deep into the natural beauty of Forever Florida, a 4,700-acre eco-ranch and wildlife conservation area where Mother Nature is king. Choose from one of the many guided adventures, or get a view from above as you soar among the treetops on the Zipline Safari.

  5. The Amway Center: Take a quick trip to Orlando and score tickets to the hottest shows in town. April features the Orlando Magic making a late season push toward the NBA playoffs and a UFC event on April 19, for those in more of a fighting mood.
 
 

ISM: Kissimmee Gateway Airport


Kissimmee Gateway Airport is Florida's entry point to fun. Each year, ISM welcomes visiting tourists from around the world looking to take in Florida's famous sunshine and attractions.

ISM is the closest general aviation facility to Walt Disney World, Sea World, Universal Studios Orlando and the Orlando-Orange County Convention Center. Located less than 5 miles from downtown Kissimmee, ISM is near many of Florida's major thoroughfares, which will quickly connect you to Orlando and many top attractions. ISM welcomes private jets and small commercial aircraft.

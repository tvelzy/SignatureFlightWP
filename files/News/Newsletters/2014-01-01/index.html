What's on in Boca Raton

 

Located along Florida's Gold Coast, Boca Raton is an inviting, palm tree-lined locale where leisure is a way of life. Featuring some of the Sunshine State's most elegant hotels, spas and restaurants, "Boca" is a great place to kick back, unwind and relax in the sun. Tourists flock to the many top resorts that call Boca Raton home, including the Waldorf Astoria's luxurious and sophisticated Boca Raton Resort and Club. And when not enjoying the many beaches nearby, visitors can enjoy world class dining and shopping at downtown's Mizner Park or catch an outdoor show at Sunset Cove Amphitheater.

 
 
 
 

Things you can do:

  1. Mizner Park: This open-air shopping mall is also the center of Boca's nightlife scene. Settle in at the swanky iPic Theaters, which offers pillows, blankets and in-film food service.

  2. Delray Beach: With more than 100 restaurants, shops and art galleries, a laid-back day at one of Florida's most popular beaches can stretch into the night.

  3. Boca Museum of Art: Need your art fix? Here you'll find works from Picasso, Degas and Matisse - Oh my!

  4. Sugar Sand Park: Seeking fun for the family? Sugar Sand offers a colossal fort, splash zone, carousel and a children's science museum – perfect for children and your own inner kid.

  5. Sunset Cove Amphitheater: Located along the waters of Sunset Cove, this is the largest in the Palm Beach County trio of theaters. Attend one of two live performances in January.
 
 

BCT: Boca Raton Airport

Established in 1936, Boca Raton Airport is a state-owned general aviation airport conveniently located just two miles from Boca Raton's central business district and immediately adjacent to Interstate 95. It also serves passengers visiting the nearby destinations of West Palm Beach and Fort Lauderdale. Boca Raton Airport has rental car facilities onsite, and serves both private and small corporate airline travel.

What's on in Minneapolis

 

Minneapolis is legendary for its charm and welcoming hospitality, so it's no surprise that Signature's Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport location took first place in Pro Pilot magazine's 2014 PRASE survey, which ranks the nation's best FBO's. We congratulate the team on this well-deserved honor! Not only can you get great service when you're here, but there is no shortage of sights to see in this Midwestern gem of a city.

 
 
 
 

Top Attractions in Minneapolis:

  1. Lake Calhoun: In the land of 10,000 lakes, you can't walk five feet without running into a body of water. One of the most popular is Lake Calhoun, a local favorite among outdoor enthusiasts.

  2. Target Field: After a long Minnesota winter, there's nothing quite like a day in the sun. One of the newest ballparks in professional baseball, Target Field is the perfect place to spend an afternoon rooting on the hometown Twins.

  3. The Mall of America: In nearby Bloomington you'll find the Mall of America, the largest shopping mall in North America. With over 400 stores spread out over four levels, visitors can literally shop until they drop.

  4. Minneapolis Institute of Arts: The crown jewel of the Minneapolis arts scene, this free museum houses over 80,000 works of art, including a world-renowned Matisse exhibit.
 
 

MSP: Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport


Along with award-winning service at our MSP location, you'll find an airport that is one of the largest commercial centers between the East and West coasts. With two terminals, and connecting trains at the airport that run nonstop, you can get in and out of MSP quickly. MSP is also conveniently located next to the Mall of America and within 10 miles of both downtown Minneapolis and downtown St. Paul.

 

Signature MSP: Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport Features:

  • US Customs onsite

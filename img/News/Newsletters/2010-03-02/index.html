This month: Nice, France

Nice is the high flying, cosmopolitan capital of the French Riviera (Côte d'Azur), with over 430,000 inhabitants who enjoy its 300 days of sunshine each year. It is the fifth largest city and has the second busiest airport in France.

NCE - LFMN: Nice Côte d'Azur Airport

Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (Aéroport Nice Côte d'Azur) is located 3.2 NM (5.9 km; 3.7 mi) southwest of Nice, in the Alpes-Maritimes department of France.
The airport was built on 380 hectares of reclaimed land in 1969 and was the largest building project of its time, taking almost ten years to complete.

This month: Bozeman, Montana

Bozeman is located in Gallatin County, home to Yellowstone National Park and Montana State University. This diverse town lies nestled in a beautiful valley, surrounded by mountains. Bozeman has become a major tourism and agriculture center and its eclectic population ranges from ranchers to professors to ski enthusiasts to entrepreneurs.

BZN: Gallatin Field Airport

Southwest Montana's Gallatin Field Airport (BZN) is the only airport serving as a year-round gateway for two Yellowstone National Park entrances. It also serves the business centers of Bozeman, Belgrade and Livingston, as well as the recreation areas of Big Sky Resort, Moonlight Basin and the Bridger Bowl Ski Area.

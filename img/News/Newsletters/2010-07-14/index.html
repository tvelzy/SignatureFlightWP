EDI - EGPH: Edinburgh Intl. Airport

Edinburgh Airport is Scotland's capital airport and is within 15 minutes drive of the city center. Edinburgh itself is the gateway to the Kingdom of Fife, the Central Belt and the beautiful Scottish Borders. With an abundance of Scottish history, breathtaking scenery and numerous championship golf courses, Edinburgh has much to offer.

ABZ - EGPD: Aberdeen Intl. Airport

Aberdeen Airport has been described as the gateway to Europe's energy capital. It boasts the largest commercial heliport in the world, transporting more than 500,000 passengers in support of the North Sea oil and gas industry. The airport is ideally situated with Royal Deeside, the Grampian Mountains and the spectacular northeast Scottish coast only a short drive away.

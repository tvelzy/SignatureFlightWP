This month: Morristown, NJ

Morristown offers a wide range of amenities, historic attractions and museums including the Morris Museum and the MacCulloch Hall Museum and Gardens. Having served as George Washington's headquarters during the American Revolutionary War, a visit to Washington's Headquarters Museum is recommended. The National Trust for Historic Preservation actually named Morristown one of its "Dozen Distinctive Destinations".

MMU: Morristown Municipal Airport

Morristown Municipal Airport was established in 1929 and was originally home to the Country Aviation Club managed by the celebrated Clarence D. Chamberlin, who after Charles A. Lindbergh was the second person to fly across the Atlantic Ocean. Today it is the second-busiest general aviation airport in New Jersey and acts as a gateway to New York City situated only 27 miles / 43 kilometers away.
